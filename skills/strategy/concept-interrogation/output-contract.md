# Output Contract

## Expected Outputs

The skill must produce `A-002 Interrogated Concept Brief`.

The brief should include:

- immediate strategic viability concerns
- hidden behavioral risks
- hidden structural risks
- infrastructural dependencies
- external gatekeepers or architectural constraints
- clarifying questions needed to reduce ambiguity
- fundamental systemic transformation framing
- minimal Wedge scope
- Contradiction Index preserving unresolved tensions and known unknowns

## Artifact Expectations

The output artifact must distinguish:

- interactive interrogation findings
- formal Concept Interrogation Map content
- resolved assumptions
- unresolved contradictions
- downstream Wedge Definition inputs

The artifact may support `PT-003 Wedge Definition`, but it must not silently
rewrite the Process Tree boundary between `PT-002` and `PT-003`.

## Validation Expectations

Validation should confirm:

- source prompts remain verbatim under `source/`
- the output challenges the concept instead of prematurely validating it
- unresolved strategic tensions are preserved rather than forced into false
  resolution
- Wedge scope is minimal and explicitly bounded
- external constraints and dependencies are identified
- outputs are suitable for downstream Wedge Definition and Strategic Charter
  work
